Frequently Asked Questions

What is Grace.AI?

Grace.AI is a digital resource designed to provide young adults with biblical insights for everyday questions and challenges through the lens of Reformed theology.

Is Grace.AI a replacement for pastoral counseling?

No, Grace.AI is a supplementary tool for biblical exploration and study. For personal spiritual guidance, we always recommend connecting with your local church leadership.

How can I provide feedback about Grace.AI?

We welcome your feedback! Please use the contact form on this page or email us directly with your thoughts and suggestions.
