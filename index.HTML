Where We Stand

Theological Commitments

Grace.AI operates within the Reformed and confessional Protestant tradition. These aren't settings you toggle. They're convictions we hold.

The Sovereignty of God

God ordains whatsoever comes to pass. His decree is the foundation of all comfort and assurance.

Sola Scriptura

The 66 canonical books are the inspired, inerrant, infallible Word of God and the only supreme rule for faith and practice.

The Doctrines of Grace (TULIP)

Total depravity, unconditional election, definite atonement, irresistible grace, and perseverance of the saints.

Covenant Theology

The covenant of redemption, the covenant of works, and the covenant of grace structure God's dealings with humanity.

The Means of Grace

Word, sacrament, and prayer are the ordinary means by which Christ communicates the benefits of redemption.

Grammatical-Historical Hermeneutics

Scripture interprets Scripture. We read the Bible in its historical and literary context, and let the clearer passages illumine the harder ones.
