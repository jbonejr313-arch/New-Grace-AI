About Grace.AI

Helping young adults navigate Scripture with theological depth and practical application.

Our Mission

Grace.AI exists to help young adults navigate Scripture with theological depth and practical application. Through AI-powered biblical insights, we aim to bridge the gap between Reformed theology and the complex questions facing college students and young professionals today.

Theological Foundation

Our responses are grounded in Reformed theology, emphasizing:

  • Sola Scriptura Scripture is our highest authority
  • God's Sovereignty The Lord is in control of all things
  • Grace Alone Salvation is entirely God's work, not ours
  • Biblical Truth Applying God's unchanging Word to today's challenges

Technology

Grace.AI combines:

  • ESV Bible API Direct Scripture access for verse display and cross-references
  • Advanced AI Language models guided by confessional Reformed theology
  • Study Tools Multi-day study builder with observation, interpretation, and application
  • Mobile-first Design Built to feel native on your phone, installable as a PWA

Our Purpose

Young adults often face challenging questions about faith, purpose, relationships, and career decisions. Grace.AI provides biblically grounded perspectives to help navigate these decisions with wisdom.

We believe that Scripture has the answers to life's most important questions, and we're here to make those insights accessible to the next generation.
